You can get started by following these four simple steps

1-search:

It's very simple. Search for the property of your choice in the search box above. You can also apply basic filters on your search queries to fit your needs. Alternatively, click on the "find properties" link on the top menu to get more filters.

2-locate:

Get to see where the property is located by clicking on the image of the property you're interested in. There, you will find a map of the house address.

3-rent:

Rent the property directly from the property details page by clicking on the RENT button to connect to the property agent. We also follow up on the application to make sure you get a reply on time

4-register:

House owners and real estate agents can request for their property to be listed by clicking on the register link on the top bar. This will lead you to a form that you must fill correctly. Please be informed that the registration process can take three working days.
